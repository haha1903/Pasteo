# Privacy Policy for Pasteo

**Last Updated:** November 22, 2025

## Introduction

Pasteo ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how Pasteo handles your data.

## Data Collection

**Pasteo does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data outside of your Mac.**

## Clipboard Data

- All clipboard history is stored locally on your device
- Clipboard data never leaves your Mac
- Pasteo does not upload, sync, or share your clipboard content with any servers or third parties
- All data is stored in your local application container

## Permissions

Pasteo requires the following permissions:

### Clipboard Access
- Required to monitor and store clipboard history
- Used only to provide core clipboard management functionality

### Accessibility (Optional)
- If enabled by user, allows automatic pasting
- Never used to monitor other applications
- Can be disabled in macOS System Settings

## Security

- Pasteo runs in the macOS App Sandbox for enhanced security
- All data is protected by macOS file system permissions
- Hardened Runtime is enabled for additional protection
- You can exclude sensitive applications from being monitored

## Analytics and Tracking

**Pasteo does not use any analytics or tracking services.**

- No usage statistics are collected
- No crash reports are sent automatically
- No third-party tracking tools are integrated

## Third-Party Services

Pasteo does not integrate with or share data with any third-party services.

## Data Retention

- Clipboard history is retained according to your configured settings
- You can clear all data at any time from within the app
- Uninstalling the app removes all stored data

## Children's Privacy

Pasteo does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

You have the right to:
- Access your data (all stored locally on your Mac)
- Delete your data (clear history feature)

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your data at all times.
